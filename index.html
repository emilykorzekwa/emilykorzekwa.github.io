Featured Work

Our Lady of Guadalupe

In the 16th century the image of Mary, the Mother of God, as Our Lady of Guadalupe appeared on the tilma of Saint Juan Diego as part of a sign to the bishop of Mexico City that he should build a church at the site of the apparition. The original image is enshrined in the Minor Basilica of Our Lady of Guadalupe in Mexico City. Her feast day is the date of the apparition, December 12th, and she is the patron saint of Mexico and the Americas.

Colored pencil and ink on bristol paper

8 in X 10 in

2017
