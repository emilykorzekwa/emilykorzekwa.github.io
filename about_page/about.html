About the Artist

Artist Bio

Emily Korzekwa was born and raised in Fort Worth, Texas. She graduated from The University of Texas at Dallas in May of 2017. Over the years she has developed her skills as an artist by drawing and painting. She likes making art in series, and recently she has been drawing saints.

Emily also likes spending time with her friends and family, playing games, and learning how to cook and bake from scratch. She has a variety of other interests such as history, gardening, philosophy, and languages. Although she is not an expert in these topics, she does enjoy listening to podcasts about them in her free time and while she creates her art.
