Subheading for Light Themes

Subheadings use a sans-serif font, are light purple, and are bordered on the bottom by a thin light yellow line.

Paragraphs use a serif font, are smaller than the heading, and are dark gray.

Subheading for Dark Themes

Subheadings use a sans-serif font, are dark yellow, and are bordered on the bottom by a thin dark red line.

Paragraphs use a serif font, are smaller than the heading, and are light gray.
