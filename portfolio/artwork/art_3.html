Saint Cecilia

Saint Cecilia is the patron saint of music, so I drew her holding a lyre. She was also a Roman woman of noble house in the 2nd century and she is depicted wearing the clothes and jewelry she probably would have worn. The palm branch she holds is a symbol of her martyrdom. A print of this work is available on my Etsy shop.

Colored pencil and ink on bristol paper

8 in X 10 in
